I’m a postdoctoral researcher working on multi-agentic AI for medicine - specifically at the intersection of computer vision, natural language processing, and clinical decision support.

My broader goal is to build AI systems that help us better understand and improve human (and animal) lives, by making machine learning not only powerful, but also transparent and trustworthy.

I earned my PhD from EPFL, where I focused on pose estimation, tracking, self-supervised learning, and hierarchical action segmentation. I’ve also spent time at NAVER Labs Europe working on 3D foundation models for real-time navigation.
